First Year Research
December 2023
Recently, I completed my the 3 semester FRI program offered at Binghamton University. During my time in the FRI program, my group of five other people explored the biases presented in tech. Specifically, we delve into the racial biases present in facial recognition(FR). After identifying the flaws of FR, we assembled a neural network that identified faces more accurate than current models. Our findings and improvements were presented in a three thousand word paper and at a poster expo along with other FRI students, professors and faculty.
